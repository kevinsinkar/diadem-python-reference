# SeismicSignalNoiseRatio

!!! abstract "Variable &middot; `VarOff.chm`"
    Variable: SeismicSignalNoiseRatio

Specifies the ratio of short-term average to long-term average. The ratio of the two mean values must exceed a threshold for DIAdem to detect the P waves of a seismic event.
